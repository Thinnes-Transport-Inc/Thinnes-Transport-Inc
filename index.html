Comprehensive FTL and LTL Freight Services

At Thinnes Transport, we understand that every business has unique logistics needs. That’s why we offer both Full Trailer Load (FTL) and Less Than Truckload (LTL) services to provide flexible solutions for different shipment sizes. Our FTL service is perfect for businesses with large cargo shipments that require the entire capacity of a trailer. With FTL, clients can enjoy exclusive use of a dedicated freight vehicle, with guaranteed next-day delivery within the Chicagoland and Southern Wisconsin areas. This option provides peace of mind for companies needing prompt, safe delivery of bulk goods.

For smaller shipments, our LTL service offers a cost-effective and efficient alternative. By sharing trailer space with other shipments, clients can reduce expenses without sacrificing quality or safety. Each LTL shipment is handled with care to prevent damage, even with frequent loading and unloading. This service is ideal for businesses looking to transport smaller quantities of cargo on a regular basis. Whether you need FTL or LTL shipping, Thinnes Transport ensures every delivery is handled with the same level of professionalism and attention to detail.
